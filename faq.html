At Jani, we take pride in transforming spaces into stunning, functional works of art. Over the years, we've completed over 500 projects, consistently exceeding our clients' expectations. Our portfolio showcases a wide range of styles, from modern elegance to rustic charm, each reflecting our commitment to quality and innovation.

Our success is built on the satisfaction of our clients, who trust us to bring their visions to life with creativity and precision.

How do you handle budget constraints?

We work closely with you to establish a realistic budget during the consultation phase. Throughout the project, we prioritize transparency and communication to ensure we stay within your financial parameters while delivering high-quality results.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services including interior design consultation, space planning, furniture selection, color scheme development, decor sourcing, and project management from concept to completion.

Do you offer sustainable design options?

Yes, we are committed to sustainable design practices. We integrate eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient solutions, and sustainable practices whenever possible to reduce environmental impact.

What are your service prices?

At Jani, our service prices are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client and project. We believe in providing personalized design solutions, so our pricing varies based on factors such as project scope, design complexity, materials used, and the time required for completion. We offer a range of packages that can be customized to fit different budgets, from basic consultations to full-service interior design projects. To provide you with an accurate quote, we start with an initial consultation where we discuss your vision, preferences, and specific requirements. This helps us understand the extent of work involved and allows us to offer a detailed, transparent pricing structure. Our goal is to ensure that you receive exceptional value and results that exceed your expectations.

Do you provide custom furniture?

Yes, Jani offers custom furniture design as part of our comprehensive interior design services. We understand that every space is unique, and sometimes custom furniture is the best solution to achieve the perfect fit, style, and functionality. Our designers work closely with skilled craftsmen and manufacturers to create bespoke pieces that meet your specific requirements.

Who are your experts?

Our team at Jani consists of highly skilled and passionate design professionals with diverse backgrounds in interior design, architecture, and project management. Each expert brings a wealth of experience and a unique perspective to the table, allowing us to tackle a wide range of design challenges creatively and effectively.

How long does a project take?

The duration of a project at Jani depends on its complexity, scale, and specific requirements. Generally, smaller projects such as single-room makeovers or consultations can take a few weeks, while larger projects like full home renovations or commercial space designs can span several months. Our process begins with an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by the design phase, where we create detailed plans and select materials.

Do you handle commercial projects?

Yes, at Jani, we specialize in both residential and commercial interior design projects. Our commercial services include designing office spaces, retail environments, hospitality interiors, and more. We understand the unique challenges and requirements of commercial spaces, such as functionality, brand alignment, and customer experience.
